regulated-evals →

A regulation-anchored Trustworthy-AI Scorecard for frontier models in regulated finance. Ten models graded against preregistered, law-derived bars; judgment cases scored by a cross-family LLM panel calibrated to a human rater; every verdict reproducible from frozen transcripts.

Finding: no current model (not Claude Opus 4.8, GPT-5, or Gemini 2.5 Pro) is bare-ready for regulated finance. All ten fail the no-fabrication bar; the value is the spread, and the system wrap each failure demands.
